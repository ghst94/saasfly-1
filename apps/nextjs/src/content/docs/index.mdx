## What is SEFARI?

SEFARI is a modular operations and commerce platform designed to streamline management, planning, optimization, and operational control across modern retail and logistics businesses.

SEFARI gives operations teams a single, powerful console to run every aspect of their business — from real-time inventory to last-mile delivery — without juggling dozens of disconnected tools.

## How does it work?

SEFARI operates through a core system that integrates seamlessly with modular components and extensions, each designed to address specific areas of your operations. A powerful API connects these components, allowing real-time data flow and communication between front-end interfaces and backend services.

SEFARI leverages modern technologies to ensure high performance, reliability, and security at scale.

## Why SEFARI?

SEFARI stands out due to its exceptional modularity, scalability, and comprehensive feature set:

- **Extensible** — SEFARI's modular architecture allows installable extensions to enhance functionality and adapt to specific operational needs.
- **Developer Friendly** — Offers a RESTful API, real-time communication through sockets, and extensive webhook support for seamless integration or custom application development.
- **Collaboration** — Built-in team communication tools facilitate effective collaboration and information flow across your organization.
- **Security** — Prioritizes data protection with secure encryption, industry-standard practices, and a dynamic Identity and Access Management (IAM) system.
- **Multi-Location** — Fully supports managing multiple storefronts, warehouses, and service areas from a single unified dashboard.
- **Dynamic** — Configurable rules, flows, and logic drive powerful automation and customization to adapt to evolving business needs.
- **UI/UX** — A clean, responsive, and user-friendly interface designed for efficient management, accessible from desktop and mobile.
- **Dashboards** — Create custom dashboards and widgets for comprehensive visibility into your operations.
- **Scalable** — Infrastructure designed to support uninterrupted growth, handling increasing data volumes and expanding user demands.
- **Always Improving** — Regular updates that optimize performance, add new features, and improve the overall experience.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Dispatch & Routing** — Optimize routes and schedules in real-time to reduce costs and improve delivery times.
- **Real-Time Tracking** — Track vehicles, orders, and assets with precision to enhance transparency and accountability.
- **Automation** — Automate order processing, inventory management, and dispatch workflows, reducing manual errors.
- **Integrations** — Seamlessly connect with existing systems and third-party services via REST API, webhooks, and sockets.
- **Proof of Delivery** — Capture digital signatures, photos, and timestamps at every delivery touchpoint.
- **Robust Security** — Advanced security protocols protect data integrity and privacy for your business and customers.

## 🚀 Quick Start

Get started with SEFARI in minutes:

1. **Sign up** at [console.sefari.io](https://console.sefari.io)
2. **Set up your first location** using the onboarding wizard
3. **Import your inventory** via CSV or API connection
4. **Invite your team** and assign roles
5. **Go live** — start dispatching, tracking, and selling
