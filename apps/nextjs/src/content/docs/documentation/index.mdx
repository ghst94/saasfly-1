## Platform Overview

### Unified Operations Dashboard

Plan, dispatch, and manage your entire operation from a single real-time dashboard. Track every driver, route, and order at a glance across all your locations.

### AI-Powered Dispatch & Routing

Reduce delivery costs by up to 45% with intelligent route optimization. SEFARI's AI engine handles automated dispatch and accurate ETA calculation, so your team can focus on what matters.

### Real-Time Fleet Tracking

Live driver locations, precise ETAs, and proof-of-delivery capture — all synced in real time. Customers are kept informed automatically via SMS and push notifications.

### Modular & Extensible

Install extensions to unlock new capabilities, or build your own using SEFARI's developer toolkit. Connect any platform via webhooks, sockets, or our REST API.
