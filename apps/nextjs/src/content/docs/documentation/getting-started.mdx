## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A verified email address to create your SEFARI account
- Your business information ready (name, address, locations)
- If integrating via API: familiarity with REST APIs and webhooks

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up at [console.sefari.io](https://console.sefari.io). You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.

## Step 2: Set Up Your First Location

After signing in, the onboarding wizard will guide you through:

1. **Business details** — Name, address, contact information
2. **Location setup** — Add your first storefront or warehouse
3. **Team invitations** — Invite team members and assign roles
4. **Inventory import** — Upload a CSV or connect an existing system

## Step 3: Configure Operations

Once your location is set up:

- **Dispatch settings** — Configure auto-dispatch rules and driver assignments
- **Routing preferences** — Set optimization priorities (speed vs. cost)
- **Notifications** — Enable SMS/push alerts for customers and drivers
- **Payment methods** — Connect your payment processor

## Step 4: Go Live

You're ready to start operating. From the SEFARI console you can:

- Create and assign orders
- Track drivers in real time
- Monitor inventory levels
- Generate reports and analytics

<Callout>
Need help? Use the in-app support chat from the SEFARI console at any time.
</Callout>
